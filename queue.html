Queue Operations

Interactive Simple Queue (FIFO) with Visualization

Instructions

  • Enter a value to Enqueue at rear.
  • Click Dequeue to remove from front.
  • Click Front to view front element.
  • Click Is Empty to check if queue is empty.
  • Click Size to view number of elements.
  • Click Search to find a value; it will be highlighted.
  • Click Max/Min to highlight max (red) and min (green) elements.
  • Queue is horizontal; front is left, rear is right.

Algorithm Explanation

A queue is a linear data structure following FIFO (First In First Out). Enqueue adds at rear, Dequeue removes from front.
